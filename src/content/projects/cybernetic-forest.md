# Cybernetic Forest

**Festival**: Ars Electronica 2023

## Description

A forest made of fiber optics and capacitive touch sensors. When viewers touch the "plants", they emit digital spores (particles) on the surrounding projection walls and trigger spatialized sound.

- **System**: TouchDesigner + Arduino
- **Audio**: Ambisonics 3rd Order
