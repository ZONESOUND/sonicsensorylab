# Urban Noise Harvesting

**Location**: Ximending, Taipei
**Status**: Ongoing

## Concept

Modern cities are overwhelmed by sonic pollution. *Urban Noise Harvesting* acts as a filter, recording street noise and using AI to classify and reorganize specific frequencies into harmonious chords.
